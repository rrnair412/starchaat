About Us

Our Story

STAR CHAAT CUISINE is an Indian restaurant located in Dublin, California.

Our diverse vegetarian menu includes a wide variety of flavorful dishes that are always made with fresh, quality ingredients using authentic and time-tested recipes that have been passed down generations. We cater to the true taste of India - which is pure comfort or nostalgia for some and a culinary adventure for others. There are many interesting flavors, textures, tastes and aromas to explore on our menu

Our restaurant is modern and comfortable and the food is always served with a smile. No matter the occasion or dietary restrictions, there is something at Star Chaat for everyone to enjoy.
